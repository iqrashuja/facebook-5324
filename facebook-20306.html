Such behavior may result to unfavorable consequences on psychological well-being, hindering with in-person connections and work output.

    The addiction associated with the social media platform may additionally cause a feeling of comparison and covetousness among users. Consistently viewing best moments of others' lives may result to sentiments of insufficiency and low self-esteem.
